I am first-year PhD student in the [UTN Data Systems Lab](https://www.utn.de/en/departments/department-engineering/data-systems-lab/), advised by Andreas Kipf. My main interest is in query optimization for cloud databases.

Previously, I worked as a student research assistant in the [TUM database group]("https://db.in.tum.de/") (Prof. Thomas Neumann) and in the [DAML group]("https://www.cs.cit.tum.de/en/daml/home/") (Prof. Stephan Günnemann).

During my studies I did two industry internships at Oracle Labs and Amazon Redshift.

Whenever I see a problem in other areas that is similar to a database problem, I cannot leave it unsolved (see my blog).
